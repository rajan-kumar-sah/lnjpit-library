About Lnjpit

Loknayak Jai Prakash Institute Of Technology(LNJPIT), Chhapra is a government owned engineering college in Bihar, India, ESTD in 2012. It is affiliated with the Aryabhata Knowledge University, Patna. LNJPIT, Chhapra spread over 40 acres of land. LNJPIT is the 7th government Engineering College in Bihar state. The plan is to make it a state of the art technology center and research hub. The first session commenced from 08/09/2012 with an intake of 240 students in four streams of engineering viz. Civil, Mechanical, Electrical and Electronics, and Computer science and Engineering.


About Lnjpit Library

LNJPIT LIBRARY is a Online Library Management System of Loknayak Jai Prakash Institute Of Technology(LNJPIT), Chhapra. Here Students can online search available Books, Download E-Books & notes and previous year question papers etc. Students can create their account and manage thier profile using username and password. Students Get Many Standard Features like Sequrity, Check Library is ON or OFF, Request for new Books, Send Feedback, Send Complaint, Unique User Id and Password for Login Purpose.
